# Project Plan: Chickenkings House Kerugoya Restaurant Website

## Goal:
To create a modern, responsive, and conversion-focused restaurant website for "Chickenkings House Kerugoya" that attracts customers, builds credibility, and facilitates easy ordering, calling, and visiting.

## Key Features & Pages:

1.  **Landing Page (Homepage)**:
    *   Hero section: High-quality fried chicken image, bold headline ("Best Chicken in Kerugoya – Fresh, Fast, Delicious"), subheading ("Dine-in, Takeaway & Delivery Available").
    *   Call-to-action buttons: "Order Now", "Call Us", "Get Directions".
    *   Display rating: ⭐ 5.0 (1 Review).
    *   Highlight key services: Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery.

2.  **About Section**:
    *   Business name: Chickenkings House Kerugoya
    *   Location: F7WH+X2W, Kerugoya, Kenya (Flames E-zone)
    *   Description: "A casual, family-friendly restaurant serving delicious chicken meals, perfect for lunch, dinner, and quick bites."

3.  **Menu Section**:
    *   Categories: Chicken Meals, Snacks / Quick Bites, Drinks.
    *   Placeholder images, prices, and clean card layout.

4.  **Services Section**:
    *   Icons for: Delivery, Takeaway, Dine-in, Table service.

5.  **Features / Highlights**:
    *   Good for: Lunch, Dinner, Solo dining, Groups, Tourists.
    *   Amenities: Toilet, Wheelchair-accessible toilet.
    *   Atmosphere: Casual.

6.  **Contact Section**:
    *   Phone: +254 712 042322
    *   Open hours: Open daily – Closes at 8 PM
    *   Embedded Google Map (coordinates: F7WH+X2W, Kerugoya).
    *   Buttons: "Call Now", "Get Directions".

7.  **Payments Section**:
    *   Accepted methods: Credit cards, Debit cards, NFC mobile payments.

8.  **Parking Info**:
    *   Options: Free street parking, Free parking lot.

9.  **Testimonials Section**:
    *   Include a placeholder section for testimonials.

10. **"Why Choose Us" Section**:
    *   Highlights: Fast service, Affordable prices, Quality food.

11. **Footer**:
    *   All business details and social media icons.

## Design Requirements:
*   **Color Theme**: Red, black, and white.
*   **UI/UX**: Modern, responsive, mobile-first, smooth animations, bold fonts, clean spacing.
*   **Visuals**: High-quality food images (fried chicken, fries, drinks).

## Functionality:
*   Click-to-call button.
*   WhatsApp contact button.
*   Simple order request form (name, phone, order).
*   Fast loading speed.
*   SEO optimized (keywords: "best chicken in Kerugoya", "Kerugoya restaurant").

## Development Process:
The `frontend_engineer` will be responsible for implementing this plan. They will first perform a preflight check, read this plan, then run `generate_images_bulk` before writing any files. Finally, they will validate the build.
